MY PHILOSPHY

Every wedding is unique and I have been fortunate enough to photograph all types ceremonies and celebrations in the past 9 years. My goal is to create photos in a photojournalistic and unobtrusive way to help you to remember the day as it really happened. No overly staged moments, awkward poses, or over-the-top props - just you and all the realness of your day.

THE EXPERIENCE

Before your wedding day, I will be there to help with timelines, vendor recommendations and be a sounding board for all your many ideas. On your wedding day, I will be there to keep you calm, fluff/bustle your dress, teach you how to tie a bow tie, remind you to eat and take beautiful photos.

After your wedding you can relax knowing your memories were documented and get excited knowing preview photos will be in your inbox soon!
